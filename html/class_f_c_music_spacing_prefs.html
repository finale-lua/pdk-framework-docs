◆ UNISONS_MODE

enum FCMusicSpacingPrefs::UNISONS_MODE

The constants used by GetUnisonsMode. These represent the three possible values for "Avoid Collision of Unisons" in the Document Options - Music Spacing dialog and control whether note spacing avoids collisions of unisons in different voices or layers. 

Lua-supported.
Enumerator
UNISSPACE_NONE 

Do not avoid collisions of unisions.

UNISSPACE_DIFFERENTNOTEHEADS 

Avoid collisions if the noteheads are of a different type (e.g., quarter-note vs. half-note or whole-note).

UNISSPACE_ALLNOTEHEADS 

Avoid collisions on any unisons regardless of notehead type.
